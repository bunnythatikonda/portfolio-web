This experience ignited my passion for design, architecture, and data analysis, motivating me to delve deeper into this interdisciplinary field.

My involvement in numerous science projects has sharpened my abilities in data management, analysis, visualization, design, simulation, and validation, enabling me to effectively tackle intricate engineering-driven challenges.

I am eager to leverage my engineering expertise to further my professional growth.
